How to Use

  • Edit the JSON in the textarea to customize your Pokémon card details
  • For images, you have two options:
    • Paste any image URL in the image field
    • Copy an image to your clipboard and click the "Paste Image" button
  • Click "GENERATE CARD" to create your custom card
  • Drag the card to rotate it and see the 3D effect!
  • Hover over the card to see a nice zoom effect

JSON Format: Include these fields: name, hp, types (array), attacks (array with name, cost, damage, description), weakness (type and value), resistance (type and value), retreatCost (number), artist, setId, and number.
